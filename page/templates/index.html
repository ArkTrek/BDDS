Your Path to Understanding and Health

Struggling with persistent headaches or neurological symptoms?
Our state-of-the-art web app is designed to help you better understand potential brain-related conditions and connect you to the right healthcare professionals.

By combining symptom analysis and advanced medical imaging diagnostics, we aim to provide a comprehensive assessment for potential brain diseases.
Our system leverages cutting-edge machine learning technology to analyze MRI and other scan types, offering precise insights into your health.

How It Works

  1. Analyze Your Symptoms: Fill out a simple questionnaire to evaluate the severity and type of potential brain conditions.
  2. Connect with Specialists: Get recommendations for trusted doctors based on your results.
  3. Upload Medical Scans: Provide MRI or other scan images for advanced analysis powered by machine learning.
  4. Receive Results: Access detailed reports on your condition to help guide your next steps.

Our mission is to empower patients with the tools they need to take control of their health, backed by accurate diagnostics and professional guidance.
